About...

I am a recent graduate of the University of Miami, with a degree in Broadcast Journalism. I am a Production Specialist with a focus in live sports. Creatively and technically experienced with live events varying from direction of ESPN3 events to replay operation of MLS soccer matches.


While attending college at the University of Miami, I was able to experience a wide array of live production enviroments. Starting within the University's athletic department as a production assistant, allowed other doors to be open for me professionally. Vista Worldlink is a production company that I currently freelance at, and it has given me even more experience in a live sports enviroment.


As a producer of Sportsdesk, a student run thirty minute long weekly program, I was able to sharpen more of skills, especially related to video editing. Also through this position, I was able to learn leadership and management skills, as I had to correspond and coordinate various tasks for over twenty-five students.
